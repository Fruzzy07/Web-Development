Absolutely Fabulous Greek

  • Time: 10
  • Serves: 120
Ingredients Quantity
olive oil 1,5 quarts
garlic powder 1/3 cup
dried oregano 1/3 cup
dried basil 1/3 cup
pepper 1/4 cup
salt1/4 cup
onion powder1/4 cup
Dijon-style mustard1/4 cup
red wine vinegar 2 quarts

Step 1: Mix olive oil, garlic powder, oregano, basil, pepper, salt, onion powder, and Dijon-style mustard together in a very large container.
Step 2: Pour in vinegar slowly while mixing vigorously until well blended. Store tightly covered at room temperature.

French Greek Salad Dressing

  • Time: 10
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Greek seasoning 1 tablespoon
water 1 tablespoon
kosher salt 1/4 teaspoon
olive oil 1/3 cup
wine vinegar 1/4 cup
lemon juice2 tablespoons
grated lemon zest1 teaspoon
Dijon mustard1/4 cup
ground black pepper 1/4 teaspoon
white sugar 1/4 teaspoon

Step 1: Stir Greek seasoning and water together in a small bowl; let stand until seasoning is moistened, about 5 minutes.
Step 2: Crush the garlic clove with the side of a knife; sprinkle kosher salt over garlic and mash garlic and salt together into a paste with the edge of the knife.
Step 3: Whisk garlic mixture, olive oil, wine vinegar, lemon juice, lemon zest, Dijon mustard, pepper, and sugar into Greek seasoning mixture until combined.
